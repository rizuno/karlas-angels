COFFEE | Check our Coffee page!

CAPPUCCINO

A Cappuccino is composed of a single espresso shot and hot milk, with the surface topped with foamed milk.

MACCHIATO

For a Hot Macchiato it is composed of an added layer of steamed milk, topped with two shots of espresso, then finished with milk foam.

ESPRESSO

An Espresso is known for its raw strong taste and acidity. When added with steamed milk, it becomes Cappuccino or Latte.


TEA | Check our Tea page!

GREEN TEA

Green Tea is brewed from dried leaves of Camellia sinensis but it should be unoxidized to preserve antioxidants.

LEMON TEA

Prepared from Black Tea or Green Tea, Lemon Tea is a hot drink added with the right amount of lemon juice. The amount is based from the person's preference.

GINGER TEA

Ginger Tea has a sweet spicy flavour coming from the roots of a Ginger plant. Mostly prepared by steeping the ginger in hot water to extract flavour.


SHAKE | Check our Shake page!

MANGO SHAKE

Mango Shake is a cold drink that is simply prepared by blending ripe mango and sugar. Flavour can be increased by adding milk to have a thicker viscosity.

OREO CHEESECAKE

The Oreo Cheesecake is a thick, creamy cold drink that contains ice cream, cream cheese, whipped cream, topped with graham crackers and oreo cookies.

FOUR SEASONS SMOOTHIE

FOUR SEASONS smoothie is often composed with the combination of Berries, Mango, Banana, and Orange flavours. Blended together and it will result into a blast of fruity flavours.
