Macao Black Pearl MilkTea

you know a milk-tea shop’s good when they get their basics right. Their take on the classic milk tea + boba combo—ticks all the right boxes: you can definitely taste the tea (they use Ceylon Premium Black Tea), there’s a proper amount of creaminess for balance (without it taking over), and the pearls have that sought-after QQ quality.

Ingredients:
Ice, Milk, Macao Black Pearl, Macao Original Tea, Sugar, Cream

Chestnut Cream Uji Matcha

Perfect for every Matcha lovers out there. Looking for a lighter alternative to Macao Imperial’s cream-cheese topping? Try the Chestnut Cream, which is topped with crushed chestnuts that contribute a welcome nuttiness. In the Chestnut Cream Uji Matcha it is paired with a matcha base that’s as earthy as it is creamy.

Ingredients:
Ice, Milk, Macao Black Pearl, Macao Uji Matcha Flavoring, Sugar, Chestnut Cream, chestnuts

Cream Cheese Oreo Milk Tea

Macao Imperial’s Cream Cheese line differs from the Cheesecake line in that instead of having cream cheese swirled around the cup, it comes as a separate topping. In the Cream Cheese Oreo Milk Tea, crushed Oreos add a slightly dusky, chocolatey profile to the earthy-meets-savory flavor of the milk tea and cream cheese.

Ingredients:
Ice, Milk, Macao Black Pearl, Crushed Oreos, Macao Cream Cheesecake, Sugar, Cream, Chocolate, Macao Original tea
