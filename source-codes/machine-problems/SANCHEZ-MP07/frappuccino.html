Starbucks Frappuccino®

Frappuccino is a line of blended iced coffee drinks sold by Starbucks. It consists
of coffee or crème base, blended with ice and ingredients such as flavored syrups
and usually topped with whipped cream and or spices.

  • Strawberry & Caramel
  • Salted Caramel Mocha
  • Chai Crème
